DOSING DEMONSTRATION TOOL

Patient Values

Iron Stores (mg):

Note that the Ganzoni formula states that for a person with a body weight above 35 kg, the iron stores are 500 mg or above.

Target Hb:

Level may depend on aetiology, symptoms, sex, age, pregnancy, etc.

Actual Hb:

Levels must be below the target Hb value.

Ideal body weight (bw):

It is recommended using the patient’s ideal body weight.
