Disclaimer

The Monofer Dosing Tool is designed with the aim to assist healthcare professionals in providing quality treatment for patients with iron deficiency anaemia.

Please note that the Monofer Dosing Tool is not applicable for assessing the iron deficit resulting from an acute bleeding episode. Please refer to the Monofer summary of product characteristics for information regarding iron replacement in patients with blood loss.

It is intended to be an easy-to-use, practical tool to help you define optimal target treatments for one or more patients based on your clinical judgement.

While we hope you find the Monofer Dosing Tool helpful, it is stressed that it cannot replace your clinical judgement as a healthcare professional. For this reason, Pharmacosmos holds no responsibility what so ever for any potentially incorrect treatment following the usage of the tool. We ask you carefully to make sure you have selected the right options so that the tool uses the formula you intend/need to use for your patient's clinical situation.

Before prescribing Monofer or any other treatment for iron deficiency anaemia, you should always consult the local summary of product characteristics. Do not prescribe the calculated dose without first assuring from a clinical point of view that the dose is relevant and correct for your specific patient.
